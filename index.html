About

Hello and welcome! I am a Librarian/Archivist who specializes in audiovisual and digital materials. Particular interests of mine are, using technology to increase access to collections, integrating AV resources into instruction, Open Source software/workflows/formats and preserving the audiovisual heritage of the Pacific Northwest!

For my CV please see the 'Work' section of this website. For examples of some of my projects see the 'Projects' section.

Projects

Analog Sounds of Washington: An attempt to bring A/V archiving into the field while preserving the sounds of our state. Creating archival records of soundscapes using a Sony DC-5 recorder and analog tape.

Project Maintainer for:
ffmprovisr: An educational resource for the use of the FFmpeg software suite oriented towards preservation and archives minded users.

AudioRecorder: An open source audio digitizing tool built with the Library and Archives community in mind as primary users.

vrecord: A widely adopted open source tool for the archival digitzation of analog video materials.
